BigRuby 2014
Opportunity Scholarship

Apply Now!

BigRuby have partnered with Ruby Central to offer a limited number of full conference registration scholarships for BigRuby conference on 2014 Feb. 20 & 21, which is open to anyone who would not normally attend, though we specifically encourage students, women, minorities, and other under-represented groups in the technology sector to apply. The Opportunity Scholarship is a chance to jump in to learn more about Ruby specifically and software development in general. Note: the scholarship does not include any travel or hotel assistance.

Scholars will be partnered with Guides: experienced programmers who have attended BigRuby before and are already familiar with many of the key players at the conference. Guides will act as ambassadors for the duration of the conference, allowing Scholars to meet established developers, learn more about software development, and begin connecting to the Ruby community. If you or someone you know is interested in applying, please read the application instructions below.

APPLICATION PROCESS

We are offering a limited number of complimentary BigRuby 2014 conference tickets as part of this scholarship. While it is open to everyone, we'd specifically encourage students, women, minorities, and other under-represented groups in the technology sector to apply.

Please fill out the form or through the Apply link. This is your chance to show us your qualifications for the opportunity scholarship. While the application deadline is Jan 17 (11:59pm EDT), we will be reviewing and responding to applications on a rolling basis during the process, so please don't hesitate to apply early! All applications will be responded to by Jan 21th.

It is very important that you regularly check the email account that you provided us during the review process. If we follow up with an acceptance but do not receive a confirmation from you within 24 hours, your acceptance will be revoked.

AWARD BENEFITS

  • Complimentary registration
  • Pairing with a developer Guide
  • Does not include travel or lodging
